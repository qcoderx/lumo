Our Mission: A Smart Energy Ecosystem

We are building a smart energy ecosystem with four core objectives designed to tackle the energy crisis head-on.

  • Efficiency

    Maximizing energy output from every source with our unique solar enhancement sheet.

  • AI Optimization

    Using AI to predict and manage energy flow in real-time for peak performance.

  • Local Growth

    Partnering with local micro-factories to create jobs and reduce costs.

  • Accessibility

    Making clean energy affordable for everyone through modular design and leasing.

Our Technology

LUMO's innovation lies in its hardware design and intelligent software, working together to deliver superior performance.

How LUMO is Manufactured

  1. Design & Planning

    Circuit layout in KiCad, casing modeled in Blender/Fusion 360.

  2. Component Sourcing

    Microcontrollers, AI modules, sensors sourced from trusted vendors.

  3. PCB Fabrication

    Fabricated via PCBWay, DIY etching, or local makerspaces.

  4. AI Integration & Assembly

    ESP32 + lightweight AI modules assembled in an ABS casing.

  5. App Sync & Software

    Firmware installation and connection to mobile app and cloud dashboard.

Solar Enhancement Sheet

  • Engineered to concentrate ambient and low-light rays onto the panel.
  • Made from translucent material with embedded nano-lens or micro-prism arrays.
  • Incorporates photoluminescent pigments to re-emit light at optimal wavelengths.
  • Works under moonlight or street lighting for night-time trickle charging.
